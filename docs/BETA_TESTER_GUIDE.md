# WohnAgent Beta Tester Guide
## Willkommen zum Beta-Test!

Thank you for participating in the WohnAgent beta testing program! This guide will help you get started and make the most of your testing experience.

---

## 1. Getting Started / Erste Schritte

### Step 1: Join the Beta Program

1. Check your email for the invitation from WohnAgent
2. Click on the **opt-in link** in the email
3. Sign in with your Google account
4. Accept the invitation to become a tester
5. Click **Download it on Google Play**
6. Install the app on your Android device

### Step 2: Create Your Account

1. Open the WohnAgent app
2. Choose your role:
   - **Mieter (Tenant)** - Looking for an apartment
   - **Vermieter (Landlord)** - Renting out properties
3. Sign up with email or Google/Apple login
4. Complete your profile

### Step 3: Review Test Scenarios

See [BETA_TEST_SCENARIOS.md](./BETA_TEST_SCENARIOS.md) for detailed testing instructions.

## 4. Reporting Bugs

### When You Find a Bug:

1. **Take a screenshot** (Power + Volume Down)
2. **Note the steps** that caused the bug
3. **Fill out bug report** using this template:

```
Bug Title: [Short description]
Severity: Critical / High / Medium / Low

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Open app
2. Go to [screen]
3. Tap [button]
4. Bug occurs

Expected: [What should happen]
Actual: [What actually happened]

Device: [e.g., Samsung Galaxy S21]
Android Version: [e.g., Android 13]
App Version: [Check in Settings]

Screenshot: [Attach]
```

### Bug Severity Guide:

**Critical** - App crashes, cannot use core features
**High** - Important feature broken, major UI issue
**Medium** - Minor feature issue, cosmetic problem
**Low** - Small polish items, suggestions

## 8. Tips for Effective Testing

✅ **Test on your actual device** - Use it like a real user would
✅ **Try different scenarios** - Don't just follow the script
✅ **Test with real data** - Use realistic search criteria
✅ **Test offline** - What happens without internet?
✅ **Be thorough** - Check every button and link
✅ **Document everything** - Screenshots are invaluable
✅ **Test regularly** - Daily testing is most helpful
✅ **Update promptly** - Install new versions when notified

## 9. Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How much time should I spend testing?**
A: 15-30 minutes per day is ideal. Quality over quantity!

**Q: What if I find the same bug multiple times?**
A: Report it once with detailed steps. No need to report duplicates.

**Q: Can I share the app with friends?**
A: No, this is a closed beta. Only invited testers can access it.

**Q: Will my data be deleted after beta?**
A: We'll notify you before launch. You may need to create a new account.

**Q: What if the app crashes constantly?**
A: Report it immediately as a Critical bug with device details.

**Q: Can I suggest new features?**
A: Absolutely! We welcome all feedback and suggestions.

**Q: How do I update to the latest beta version?**
A: Check Google Play Store for updates, or enable auto-updates.
